>Keep Yourself Safe Online: A Prevention Checklist
Never give out your bank account information, UPI PIN, or OTP.
QR codes are used to make payments; do not scan them to get money.
Always double-check calls or messages purporting to be from businesses or banks.
Avoid clicking on dubious links and only use official apps.
Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for significant accounts.
Update your devices with the most recent security patches.
Steer clear of using public Wi-Fi for financial or banking purposes.
Check your transaction history and bank statements on a regular basis.
UPI Safety: Before authorizing any payment, confirm the recipient's name.
UPI Safety: Refuse unidentified payment requests right away
